In 2022 my company's main products were written in ASP.NET WebForms, an environment
Microsoft declared obsolete and replaced with ASP.NET Core — two frameworks that,
despite the similar name, share little more than C#.

Rewriting from scratch meant choosing the foundations for the next ten years of the
product. This project collects the evaluation work and the building of a proprietary
.NET-based framework designed for a team of fifteen developers.
