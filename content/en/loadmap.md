## Privacy First

No account to create, no ads, no tracking. Your data stays yours, in your private iCloud: not even the developer can access it. [Read the full privacy policy](/en/privacy-loadmap/).

## Truly Simple

No superfluous features: just what you need to train and see your progress. Whether you're following a plan at the gym or training for your first 10K, Loadmap shows you the road behind you — and the one ahead.
