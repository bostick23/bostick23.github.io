## Key Features

{{< rawhtml >}}
<div class="wm-features">
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">📅</span>
    <h3>The week's menu</h3>
    <p>Breakfast, lunch, snack and dinner for every member of your household, with quick add from the catalog and on-the-fly creation of missing foods.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">👨‍👩‍👧‍👦</span>
    <h3>One household, one plan</h3>
    <p>Create your household and invite family or roommates with a code: everyone sees the same calendar and the same list, in real time.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">🛒</span>
    <h3>One list, not ten sticky notes</h3>
    <p>Pick the dates and the people: Mangiamo merges identical ingredients and adds up quantities, converting units along the way (g↔kg, ml↔l).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">🏪</span>
    <h3>Sorted like your supermarket</h3>
    <p>Items grouped by department, so you walk the store once.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">✅</span>
    <h3>Built for the cart</h3>
    <p>Check off purchases, mark what's already in the pantry, add extra items. Regenerating the list never wipes your checkmarks.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wm-feature">
    <span class="wm-feature-icon">📶</span>
    <h3>Works offline too</h3>
    <p>No signal between the aisles? No problem: the list works offline and syncs as soon as you're back online.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Your Groceries Are Not for Sale

No ads, no analytics, no tracking. Sign in with your Google account and your household's data stays in your private space, visible only to the people you invite. [Read the full privacy policy](/en/privacy-mangiamo/).

## Truly Simple

No fancy recipes, no complicated nutrition plans: just the answer to every week's question — what are we eating, and what do we need to buy.
