## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how personal data is handled within the **WalletManager** mobile application (hereinafter "the App"), developed by **Claudio Bosticco** (hereinafter "the Developer").

Protecting your privacy is an absolute priority. The App was designed from the ground up to work entirely offline, without collecting, transmitting, or sharing any personal data.

## Data collected

**The App does not collect any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No data is transmitted** to external servers, cloud services, or third parties
- **No account** is required to use the App
- **No registration** or authentication is necessary
- **No analytics** or tracking services are integrated into the App
- **No cookies** or tracking technologies are used

## Data stored locally

The App allows you to record personal financial information (income, expenses, categories, wallets). All this data is stored **exclusively on your device** and under your complete control.

In particular:

- Financial data you enter remains in the device's local storage
- The backup feature exports data in JSON format to the device itself
- The Developer **has no access** to your data in any way

## Data sharing

The App **does not share any data** with third parties. There are no integrations with advertising services, analytics, social networks, or other external services.

## Data security

Since data never leaves your device, security is ensured by the iOS operating system's built-in protection mechanisms and the security measures you have configured on your device (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

## User rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

- **Access**: you can view all data entered directly in the App
- **Modification**: you can edit any data at any time
- **Deletion**: you can delete individual transactions, categories, or wallets, or uninstall the App to remove all data
- **Portability**: you can export your data using the backup feature in JSON format

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

The Developer reserves the right to update this policy. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date shown at the top.
